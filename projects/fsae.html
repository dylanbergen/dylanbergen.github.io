UW Formula Motorsports FSAE

Three years on the University of Washington Formula SAE team, progressing from manufacturing to suspension design to Race Team Lead. Machined student-designed parts on CNC equipment, designed and tested suspension components, and managed vehicle setup and on-site race support.

Team UW Formula Motorsports
Years 2016 — 2019
Roles Mfg → Suspension → Lead

Manufacturing (2016–2017)

Started on the manufacturing sub-team, machining student-designed parts using CNC mills and lathes in the UW machine shop. Learned to work from engineering drawings with GD&T callouts and produce parts to specification under deadline pressure for competition.

Suspension Design (2017–2018)

Moved into the suspension sub-team, responsible for the design, manufacture, testing, and support of suspension components. This included designing parts in CAD, performing basic structural analysis, and working with the manufacturing team to ensure designs were producible with available equipment and materials.

Race Team Lead (2018–2019)

As Race Team Lead, I was responsible for overall vehicle setup, testing sessions, maintenance, and on-site support at competition. This role required coordinating across all sub-teams, making real-time decisions about vehicle configuration, and managing the logistics of transporting and operating the car at events.

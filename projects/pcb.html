Multi-Layer PCB Design

Designing 6–8 layer mixed-technology circuit boards in KiCad, with plans for in-house SMD assembly using a custom-built reflow oven.

EDA Tool KiCad
Complexity 6–8 Layers
Technology SMD + Through-Hole

Design Work

Current projects involve 6 and 8 layer board designs with mixed SMD and through-hole components. Designs are created in KiCad, from schematic capture through layout, layer stackup definition, and design rule checks. Boards are sent to fabrication houses for manufacturing.

Assembly

Through-hole assembly is done by hand with a Hakko soldering station. For SMD assembly, I am building a custom reflow oven to handle solder paste reflow for production-quality results. The electronics lab also includes an oscilloscope for testing and debugging.
